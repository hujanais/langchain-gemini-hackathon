**Title**
Medical Assistant / Medical Assistant (m/f/d)

As a medical assistant, you will design the organizational processes in medical facilities of the Bundeswehr. You are the first point of contact for patients. Your tasks not only include making appointments, caring for patients and assisting with medical treatments, but you are also responsible for the running of the practice, the associated correspondence and for carrying out laboratory tests.

**Your tasks as a medical assistant / medical assistant (m/f/d)**

-	Use, care and maintain medical instruments, devices and apparatus based on applicable regulations and laws.
-	Carrying out the patient survey and organizing the troop doctor's consultation
-	Caring for patients.
-	Assist with treatments and examinations.
-	Carrying out and documenting laboratory work.

**In addition to your professional work, you also have other military tasks, for example:**

-	Training and instructing subordinate soldiers.
-	Assumption of material responsibility.
-	Instructing and supervising interns.
-	Participation in military training sections.

**What matters to you**

-	You work for a recognized employer in secure economic conditions.
-	You can put your interest in medical processes to good use in one of the Bundeswehr hospitals, in regional medical facilities, in the medical corps at home and abroad, in training facilities, in staffs (administrative units) at home and abroad or in command authorities and offices.
-	You work in a friendly environment.
-	You can expect a variety of additional qualification opportunities and an attractive compensation package.
